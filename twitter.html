Twitter Work

As a freelance designer and designing as a hobby, I was given a fortunate opportunity to work alongside the NBCS Bulls Talk Twitter account to create content for their page to post. I made a short video using Adobe After Effects and Adobe Premiere Pro announcing the crossover. I then made a Bulls Win graphic using Cinema 4D and Adobe After Effects. Finally, a simple final score graphic was made with Adobe Photoshop. I love to work with creative programs and working alongside a media company that creates content for my favorite basketball team was truely a blessing.
